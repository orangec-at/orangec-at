# Pricing Strategies Guide

Price for value, not cost.

## Pricing Models

### Freemium
- Free tier: Limited features
- Pro: $20-50/month
- **Example**: Notion, Vercel

### Free Trial â†’ Paid
- Trial: 7-14 days
- Pay: $29-99/month
- **Example**: Most B2B SaaS

### One-Time
- Price: $29-299
- **Example**: UI kits, templates, courses

### Usage-Based
- Pay per use
- **Example**: Vercel, AWS

## Pricing Psychology

**Anchor High**: Show $99/month tier first
**Decoy Effect**: Middle tier sells best when 3 options
**Annual Discount**: 20% off for yearly (predictable revenue)

## Starting Prices

**Developer Tools**: $20-50/month
**B2B SaaS**: $50-200/month
**Templates/UI Kits**: $29-99 one-time
**Courses**: $99-499 one-time

**Rule**: Start 2x higher than you think. Can always discount.
