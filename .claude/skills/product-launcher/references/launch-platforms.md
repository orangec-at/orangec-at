# Launch Platforms Guide

Where to launch for maximum visibility.

## Top Platforms

### 1. ProductHunt
**Best for**: Tech products, developer tools
**Timing**: Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01am PT
**Prep**: 
- Video demo
- 5 friends ready to upvote early
- Respond to every comment

### 2. HackerNews (Show HN)
**Best for**: Developer tools, technical products
**Rules**: No promotional language
**Format**: "Show HN: [Product] - [one-line description]"

### 3. Reddit
**Subreddits**: r/SideProject, r/InternetIsBeautiful, relevant niche subs
**Strategy**: Value-first posts, not spam
**Best**: Share journey/lessons with product link at end

### 4. Twitter/X
**Strategy**: Build in public for weeks before launch
**Launch day**: Thread showing journey + results

### 5. Indie Hackers
**Best for**: SaaS, dev tools
**Strategy**: Share revenue numbers for engagement

## Launch Sequence

**Day 1**: ProductHunt
**Day 2**: HackerNews
**Day 3**: Reddit
**Day 4**: Twitter thread
**Week 2**: Indie Hackers post-mortem
