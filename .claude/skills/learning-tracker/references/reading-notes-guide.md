# Reading Notes Guide

Extract value from books and articles systematically.

## Before Reading

**Ask**:
- Why am I reading this?
- What do I hope to learn?
- How will I apply it?

---

## While Reading

**Highlight + Note**:
- Key concepts
- Actionable advice
- Disagreements
- Questions

---

## After Reading

### 1. Summary (3 sentences)
- Main thesis
- Key argument
- Conclusion

### 2. Takeaways (3-5)
- Specific, actionable insights
- Each with "Action" item

### 3. Favorite Quotes (2-3)
- Most impactful passages

### 4. My Thoughts
- What resonated?
- What didn't?
- How does this connect to what I know?

### 5. Action Items
- Specific next steps
- Apply within 1 week

---
