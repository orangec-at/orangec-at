## Design Quick Reference

**Text Sizes**:
- Title: 72-120pt (bold)
- Main content: 36-48pt
- Details: 24-32pt

**Color Schemes**:
- High contrast (black on white / white on black)
- Consistent throughout carousel
- Max 3 colors total

**Layout Rules**:
- One idea per slide
- 20-30% white space
- Progress indicator (1 of 10)
- Swipe arrow on slide 1
