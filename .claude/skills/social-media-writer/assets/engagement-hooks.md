## Hook Formula Builder

**Structure**: [Attention Grabber] + [Promise/Preview]

**Attention Grabbers**:
- Surprising statistic
- Contrarian opinion
- Relatable question
- Personal confession
- Bold claim
- Specific metric

**Promise/Preview**:
- "Here's how:"
- "Here's what I learned:"
- "Here's the truth:"
- "Here's what worked:"
- "Here's my exact process:"

## Platform-Specific Tips

**Twitter**: 280 chars max, get to point fast
**Instagram**: Visual + intrigue, make them swipe
**LinkedIn**: Professional but authentic
**Facebook**: Personal story, community focus
