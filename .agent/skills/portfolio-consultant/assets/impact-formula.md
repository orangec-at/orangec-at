## STAR Method (Situation-Task-Action-Result)

### Template
\`\`\`
**Situation**: [Context - what was the problem?]
**Task**: [Your responsibility - what needed to be done?]
**Action**: [What you did - specific steps taken]
**Result**: [Quantified outcome - metrics and impact]
\`\`\`

### Example
\`\`\`
**Situation**: E-commerce site had 3.8s page load, causing 35% bounce rate
**Task**: Reduce load time to under 2s to improve conversions
**Action**: Implemented image optimization (WebP, lazy loading), code-split JavaScript bundles, added CDN
**Result**: Load time reduced to 1.2s (68% faster), bounce rate decreased to 22% (37% improvement), conversion increased from 2.3% to 3.1% (35% lift), +$12K/month revenue
\`\`\`
