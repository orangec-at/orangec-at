# Cover Letter Formulas

Write personalized, compelling cover letters that get interviews.

---

## The 3-Paragraph Structure

\`\`\`
Para 1: Hook (Why this company?)
Para 2: Fit (Why you?)
Para 3: Value (What you'll bring)
\`\`\`

---

## Paragraph 1: The Hook

### Formula
\`\`\`
[Excitement] + [Specific reason] + [Your background fit]
\`\`\`

### Templates

**Template 1: Product Love**
> I'm excited to apply for [Role] at [Company]. As a longtime user of [Product], I've been impressed by [specific feature/approach]. As a [your role] with [X years] building [type of products], I'm particularly drawn to [aspect of role].

**Template 2: Mission Alignment**
> I'm thrilled to apply for [Role] at [Company]. Your mission to [company mission] resonates deeply with my experience [relevant background]. I'm excited about the opportunity to contribute to [specific goal].

**Template 3: Recent News**
> I'm writing to express my interest in [Role] at [Company]. I was excited to see [recent funding/launch/news], and I'd love to bring my [X years] of [relevant experience] to help [Company] achieve [goal].

---

## Paragraph 2: The Fit

### Formula
\`\`\`
[Achievement 1 with metrics] + [Achievement 2 with metrics] + [Relevant skill highlight]
\`\`\`

### Template

> In my current role at [Company], I [Achievement 1 with metrics showing relevant skill]. Additionally, I [Achievement 2 demonstrating another key requirement from JD]. Most recently, I [Achievement 3 showing growth or leadership].

### Examples

**Frontend Role**:
> At TechCorp, I built a React component library with 50+ components that reduced development time by 40% across 3 products. I also optimized our e-commerce site achieving a 98 Lighthouse score, which increased conversions by 35%. Most recently, I led the migration to Next.js 14, improving our Core Web Vitals and SEO rankings.

**Backend Role**:
> In my role at DataCo, I designed and built a RESTful API serving 1M requests/day with 99.95% uptime. I optimized database queries reducing latency from 450ms to 85ms through strategic indexing. Additionally, I architected a microservices backend that scaled our system to handle 10K concurrent users.

---

## Paragraph 3: The Value

### Formula
\`\`\`
[What you'll bring] + [Enthusiasm] + [Interview request]
\`\`\`

### Template

> I'm excited about the opportunity to bring my experience with [key skill from JD] to help [Company] [specific challenge or goal from JD]. I'd love to discuss how my background in [relevant area] can contribute to [Company]'s mission to [company mission]. Thank you for considering my application.

---

## Complete Example

**Role**: Senior Frontend Engineer at Vercel

\`\`\`
Dear Hiring Manager,

I'm excited to apply for Senior Frontend Engineer at Vercel. As a developer who uses Next.js daily and contributes to the community, I've been consistently impressed by Vercel's commitment to developer experience and web performance. With 5 years building high-performance React applications, I'm particularly drawn to the opportunity to work on the platform that's shaping the future of web development.

In my current role at TechCorp, I architected a component library with 50+ reusable React components using Next.js 14 and TypeScript, reducing UI development time by 40% across our product suite. I optimized our e-commerce platform achieving a 98 Lighthouse Performance score through strategic code splitting and image optimization, which increased conversions by 35% and generated an additional $12K monthly revenue. Most recently, I spearheaded our migration to the App Router, implementing React Server Components and improving our Core Web Vitals significantly.

I'm excited about the opportunity to bring my expertise in Next.js performance optimization and component architecture to help Vercel build the tools that millions of developers rely on. I'd love to discuss how my experience scaling frontend applications can contribute to Vercel's mission to make the web faster. Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to Vercel's continued success.

Best regards,
[Name]
\`\`\`

---

## Customization Checklist

Before sending:

- [ ] **Company name** appears 3+ times
- [ ] **Specific product/feature** mentioned
- [ ] **Role requirements** addressed
- [ ] **Metrics** included in achievements
- [ ] **Keywords from JD** naturally used
- [ ] **Enthusiasm** genuine, not generic
- [ ] **Call to action** clear
