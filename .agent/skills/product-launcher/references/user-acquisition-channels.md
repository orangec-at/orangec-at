# User Acquisition Channels

Get your first 100, then 1000 users.

## Free Channels (0-100)

### ProductHunt (Effort: High, Result: High)
- One-time spike
- 50-500 signups if done well
- Lifetime badge

### Reddit (Effort: Medium, Result: Medium)
- Find 5 relevant subreddits
- Contribute before promoting
- Post as "I built X to solve Y"

### Twitter/X (Effort: Low, Result: Slow but compounding)
- #buildinpublic daily updates
- Engage with similar builders
- 0-100 users over 3-6 months

### Direct Outreach (Effort: High, Result: Immediate)
- Email 50 people who fit target user
- Personalized messages
- 10-20% conversion to trial

## Paid Channels (100-1000)

### Google Ads
- $10-30/day
- Target buyer keywords
- $20-50 cost per signup

### Facebook/Instagram Ads
- $20/day minimum
- Lookalike audiences
- Good for consumer apps

### Reddit Ads
- Cheaper than Facebook
- Target specific subreddits
- $0.50-2 CPC

## Content Marketing (Long-term)

- SEO blog posts
- YouTube tutorials
- Guest posts
- Podcast appearances

**Timeline**: 6-12 months to see results
